Instrument Blog

Creation

I created this instrument by cutting a piece of plywood into a triangle then cutting a smaller triangle out of the middle. I then drilled two series of holes and painted it. I strung rubber bands through the holes and held them in with a roll of scratch paper. I then suspended one side of the rubber bands to give them more room to vibrate, and then I was done.
